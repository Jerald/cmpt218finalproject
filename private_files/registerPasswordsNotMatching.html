The Background

Welcome to assignment 4! Here you can play 3D tic-tac-toe with another user. Upon visiting the site, please register an account then login. When logged in you have the option to start a new game, logout, or view hiscores. You will also be presented with your current stats. When you initiate a game you will sit in the lobby waiting for another player to connect. Then you both will be brought in to play a game together.

Several features have been added to have give users a better experience. A hiscores page has been added where you can compare your stats with another players. New players are unable to enter a date of birth past today's date. A sleek design has been implemented to provide a better visual experience

Thank you for playing!
